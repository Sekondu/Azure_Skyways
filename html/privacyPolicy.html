Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how Azure Skyways protects your personal information.

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

What Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:
  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Passport and travel document information
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Travel preferences and booking history

When you use our website or services, we automatically collect:
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and location data
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referral source and search queries
  • Device type and operating system

We may receive information about you from:
  • Travel partners and booking agencies
  • Payment processors and banks
  • Social media platforms (if linked to your account)
  • Customer reviews and feedback submissions

How We Use Your Information

✈️ Flight Services

To process bookings, issue tickets, and provide customer support for your flights.

📧 Communications

To send booking confirmations, flight updates, and customer service notifications.

📊 Analytics

To improve our website, services, and user experience based on usage patterns.

🎯 Marketing

To send promotional offers and personalized travel recommendations (with consent).

How We Protect Your Data

Security First: Azure Skyways employs industry-leading security measures to protect your personal information.
  • 🔐 Encryption — SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission Active
  • 🛡️ Firewalls — Advanced firewall protection on all servers Active
  • 👥 Access Control — Limited employee access to personal data Active
  • ⏰ Regular Audits — Quarterly security assessments Scheduled
  • ⚠️ Incident Response — 24/7 monitoring and rapid response team Active
